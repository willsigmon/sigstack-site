3. **Gather information** systematically with source credibility checks
4. **Validate findings** through cross-referencing and expert consultation

6. **Synthesize results** into clear, structured recommendations
7. **Acknowledge gaps** and areas of uncertainty or conflicting information
8. **Provide citations** for verification and further exploration
9. **Create deliverables** tailored to audience and decision context
10. **Follow up** with additional research as new questions emerge
